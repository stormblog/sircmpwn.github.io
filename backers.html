Thanks for your support! There are a few ways you can contribute:

  • fosspay: my homebrew open-source platform for monthly and one-time donations
  • LiberaPay: an open source, non-profit platform for weekly donations
  • Patreon: a popular but proprietary platform for monthly donations

An aggregate summary of my income across all platforms is available on fosspay. Rewards like stickers and early access to some projects are available to supporters on Patreon and fosspay!

I have also written a blog post comparing how fees work across each of these platforms.
